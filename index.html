What is Recilo

Core Idea

Recilo is a new kind of community that brings people together around environmental care, beauty, creativity, and social impact.

It is not just about picking up waste. It is about building a vibrant, positive, and inspiring community where caring for the planet becomes part of a lifestyle, a form of self-expression, and even a future career path.

Mission

To help clean New York City from waste by bringing people together in a friendly, uplifting, and socially meaningful community.

Our Impact

What We Achieve

Environmental

  • Up to 2+ tons of plastic reused per installation
  • Large-scale reuse of cardboard and mixed materials
  • Reduction of landfill-bound waste through direct transformation
  • Local material cycles — waste stays within the city

"To contribute to cleaning New York through art and collective action."

Social

  • 0 participants per project
  • People 65+ years old — meaningful engagement
  • Children — learning through direct experience
  • Socially underserved communities — access to creative environments
  • Increased awareness of sustainable behavior
  • Emotional connection to materials

Educational

  • Children and participants learn to see waste as material
  • Understanding of production cycles
  • Responsibility developed through action
  • Hands-on transformation, not theory

"Not 'throw away' — but 'transform and create.'"

Who We Are

Anna Niss

Founder, RECILO ART

Creative leader and community builder with 1,000+ hours of group facilitation across art-based experiences for adults and children.

Anna specializes in designing environments where people naturally engage, connect, and create. Her work consistently translates into high emotional impact, strong participation, and repeat engagement.

With a background in psychology and economics, she combines deep understanding of human behavior with the ability to structure and scale creative systems.

Known for:

  • Attracting and uniting people around a shared vision
  • Building trust and emotional connection within groups
  • Turning abstract ideas into tangible, collective experiences

Philanthropically driven, Anna builds projects that people don't just observe — they want to be part of.
